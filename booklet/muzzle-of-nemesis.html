When I made the Third Period, I prepared four endings in this world. No matter what progress they would follow, either of the four would arrive in the end. That is the rule, and in practice it went exactly that way. It was the absolute worst ending among the four. Well, I was fine with that. But I wasn’t pleased with the fact that there is a part of this ending that was led along by a specific being. Intervention towards her ending would be a clear breach of the rules. Try as I might I can’t accept that.

So I intend to break a rule too, just this once. That might also be feasible, if I can borrow the power of Held’s successor. How would I do that? I am the Creator. Everything that the humans of this world can do, I can do too. That’s the short of it.

“Well, it’ll be your turn before long.”
