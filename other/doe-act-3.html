The Daughter of Evil: Act 3 is a manga adaptation of The Daugher of Evil: Wiegenlied of Green released on January 27, 2016. The manga is chiefly illustrated by Ichika with some of the backgrounds illustrated by Mizutame Tori. It was preceeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 2 and succeeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 4.
