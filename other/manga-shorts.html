The manga shorts "gift from the Princess who Brought Sleep" and "Fifth Pierrot" were two-page manga shorts by Ichika based on their eponymous songs. They were released in issues of MIKU-Pack; "gift from the Princess who Brought Sleep" was released in MIKU-Pack 09 on September 13, 2014, and "Fifth Pierrot" was released in MIKU-Pack 13 on May 15, 2015.
