Aku Musu is a two-volume comedy manga series by Ken (けんken). It's part of the Comedy of Evil project alongside The Servant of Evil ~Opera Buffa!~. After being serialized in Blitz G's Comics NEXT, volume 1 was released on February 27, 2014, and volume 2 was released on January 27, 2015.
