The Daughter of Evil: Act 1

The Daughter of Evil: Act 1 is a manga adaptation of the first chapters of The Daughter of Evil: Clôture of Yellow and The Daugher of Evil: Wiegenlied of Green released on February 17, 2014. The manga is chiefly illustrated by Ichika with some of the backgrounds illustrated by Mizutame Tori. It was succeeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 2.
