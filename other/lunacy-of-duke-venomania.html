The Lunacy of Duke Venomania is a manga adaptation of the eponymous song written by mothy and illustrated by Reki Bito (尾(び)藤(とう)れき(れき)bitou reki). It focuses on elements of the story not touched upon in the light novel, particularly on the seduction of Lilien Turner. It began serialization in B's-LOG COMICS in June 2012 and was compiled into a single volume on December 28, 2012.
