The Daughter of Evil: Act 2 is a manga adaptation of the second chapter of The Daughter of Evil: Wiegenlied of Green released on December 26, 2014. The manga is chiefly illustrated by Ichika with some of the backgrounds illustrated by Mizutame Tori. It was preceeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 1 and succeeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 3.
