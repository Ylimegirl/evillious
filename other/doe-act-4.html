The Daughter of Evil: Act 4 is a manga adaptation of The Daugher of Evil: Clôture of Yellow released on March 17, 2017. The manga is chiefly illustrated by Ichika with some of the backgrounds illustrated by Mizutame Tori. It was preceeded by The Daughter of Evil: Act 3.
