The Evillious Chronicles Guide

The vessels of malice that were unleashed from the cursed twins,
What sort of story do they paint?
—Seven Crimes and Punishments

The Evillious Chronicles is a fantasy series created by mothy (Akuno-P). This site serves as a guide to the series. Check my about for more information on the website.

If I've made any mistakes or am missing information, please let me know by emailing me, messaging me on Discord (Ylimegirl#3830), or through my tumblr.
