The Evillious Chronicles:
CliffsNotes

CliffsNotes style summaries for installments in The Evillious Chronicles in release order. For event summaries in chronological order see the timeline.

2008

  • Ten Minute Love: A girl sees a boy on the bus and falls in love with him during the short ten minute ride.
  • Wordplay: A girl learns about the world through words, first joyful ones and then darker ones. She continues her wordplay in an attempt to describe her emotions.
  • Clockwork Lullaby: A girl creates the Clockwork Lullaby from words taught to her and sings it, wondering where it will reach.
  • The Daughter of Evil: A princess rules her country tyrannically for her whims. She discovers that the man she was in love with another girl, and orders the destruction of her country. The people start a revolution and capture the princess, beheading her on the guillotine at 3 o' clock.
  • The Servant of Evil: A servant swears to protect his sister the princess even if he becomes evil. He meets and falls in love with a girl, but the princess orders her death and he complies and cries. During the revolution he swaps clothes with his sister, and he is beheaded on the guillotine in her place. He wishes that if he'd want to play together again if they could be reborn.
  • Regret Message: A girl puts a message in a glass bottle, sending into the ocean hoping her wish will be granted. Remorseful of her selfish actions towards someone who is no longer here, she wishes they could be reborn.
  • Miniature Garden Girl: A paraplegic girl is doted upon by her father, only knowing the world inside her room. He brings her objects which say that they're the same as her, and she sings a lullaby for her father's sake. He says the word "war" one day and the mansion burns down, leaving behind the remains of a man and a scorched clockwork doll.
  • Tale of Abandonment on a Moonlit Night: Two twins are taken on a walk and abandoned in the woods by their parents. Using a glass bottle lit by the moon they make their way through the forest and discover the witch's house. They throw the witch and her henchman into the oven. They ask their parents to praise them and note the familiarity of the house before deciding to find their "real" parents.
  • South North Story: Two identical girls from the north and the south encounter each other at an intersection. The southern girl talks about her life as a student and the northern girl talks about her tyrannical rule. The two then part ways, hoping to somehow meet each other again.
  • Re_birthday: A boy awakens in a dark room with a large spring in the ceiling, which tells him that he'll never be released. He recalls his sins and realizes he can't return to his past and notices the red manacles and blue chains on his arms and legs. He hears a lullaby and begins to change the lyrics, and when a small light comes down from the spring he uses the lullaby to free himself from the manacles and chains. He is told he will be reborn and his surroundings turn to white.

2009

  • Evil Food Eater Conchita: Banica Conchita began seeking evil foods after once being a gourmet. Her fifteenth cook asks for a vacation, and eats him before eating her other two servants as well. After everything was gone from the mansion she realizes there's still something left to eat and devours herself.
  • EVILS THEATER: The "Collector" created a small theater in the woods before his death, with the only work screened being a documentary introducing his collection. After the theater is forgotten by everyone the springs begin moving and a voice sings; the collection pieces begin a new story with their own hands. The one acting as the theater's curator is his beloved clockwork doll.
  • moonlit bear: A woman steals two red fruits in the forest from a bear and flees home. When she shows the fruits to her husband she realizes they are two babies, and that the bear she just killed was their mother.
  • Screws, Gears, and Pride: BMD2-R meets SN7F-R after being abandoned in the C factory. During their escape they meet TYPE-L while avoiding the I Company's weapon G.
  • Heartbeat Clocktower: At a small theater in the woods, a man watches over a burnt clockwork doll kept alive by the clocktower's gears. Visitors get eaten by the master of the graveyard, while a selfish girl pretends to be the lead role. The clockwork lullaby began ringing out from the theater as they waited for the unawakened scissors and katana. The man sacrifices his heart to save the doll and becomes part of the clocktower.
  • To You of Few Words: A girl loves her quiet boyfriend, but wants him to say she loves her at least once. She learns the two can communicate even without words, and the boy suddenly says he loves her on the way home one day.
  • The Tailor of Enbizaka: A tailor is distressed by her loved one not coming home. She discovers him in town with various women as the town becomes increasingly agitated. When she hits a stopping point in her work she goes to meet him while wearing accessories she saw the women wearing. She then distresses over how he treated her like a stranger while learning that someone killed a family of four.
  • The Daughter of Evil ~velvet mix~ and The Servant of Evil ~velvet mix~ PVs: The country's king dies, and a succession war ensues; the prince is declared dead and princess becomes heir, with her twin entrusted to a royal knight. The queen dies and the princess becomes ruler. A famine breaks out in the kingdom, and a genocide begins. and a girl finds herself inside a well. The princess is deposed after a civil war breaks out.

2010

  • The Daughter of White: A girl with white hair is bullied by her green-haired peers for being different. She prays to god for a friend and finds a green haired girl collapsed in the forest. The two become friends and run away to become a merchant's wife's servants. While there they meet a foreign king, who falls in love with the green-haired girl and rejects a marriage with a princess. The princess initiates a genocide of all the green-haired women and her friend is killed, leaving the white-haired girl alone. She begins living as a nun and finds a girl collapsed by the church. The two become friends before she hears her confession declaring herself to be the princess. She takes a knife and attempts to kill the princess, but falters when she sees an illusion of a boy.
  • prelude to forest: The "collector" finds an abandoned house and builds a theater with a clocktower in its place. He discovers two skeletons when demolishing the house, and builds a graveyard next to the theater to bury them.
  • The Frog's and My Love Romance: A snake visits a spring and falls in love with a frog. The two begin a romance until the frog disappears one day, leaving the snake devastated as it eats a frog whole for dinner.
  • Full Moon Laboratory: A woman experiments on the night of the full moon and destroys in order to create. She continues her experiments to get the one thing she wants, and destroys before destroying again. She declares her goal to create that person like they once created her, and says she'll be here no matter what.
  • The Last Revolver: Santa gives a girl a revolver and is ordered to kill the one she loves. She recalls how the "evil" her met the "just" him and she fell in love even so. She apologizes as she shoots him, wishing that she could go to the festival again if she could redo it. She says she'll go see him soon and that they'll always be together, just like they always said.
  • Heartbeat Clocktower (F · V) PV: The "Collector" created a small theater in the woods before his death, with the only work screened being a documentary introducing his collection. After the theater is forgotten by everyone the springs begin moving and a voice sings; the collection pieces begin a new story with their own hands. The one acting as the theater's curator is his beloved clockwork doll.
  • EVILS FOREST: After the "Collector"'s death most of inheritance goes missing. His sole friend, the scriptwright "Ma", claims the junk collection pieces before vanishing from the public. Years later, "Ma" is sighted in the Millennium Tree Forest, and rumors spread that his inheritance is inside a small theater in that forest. Many enter the forest, but no one has returned, giving the forest the moniker "EVILS FOREST".
    "Ma" introduces herself as "the Gardener" and lists the rules of the forest before offering commentary on the works shown in the theater.
  • master of the graveyard: The Evil Food Eater was reborn as ruler of the dead and uses the graveyard as her feeding grounds. She decides to go along with the whims of the gardener for now, but will one day overtake her as she and her servants party until the eventual ending day.
  • Wendy: A girl meets Peter Pan and writes a letter full of her delusions. She jumps out the window to fly and sees a red light somewhere. She doesn't realize that everyone was laughing somewhere, and notes that she's alone as Peter Pan isn't anywhere. A flower blooms on her desk as she waves bye to people wearing black clothes and asks her parents not to cry.
  • Desert BLUEBIRD: An old woman asks a boy and his sister to search for the blue bird to save the world. The two find the blue bird inside a crumbling building, and the sister runs in alone. She finds the blue bird but has become contaminated by nuclear radiation and can't wake. The boy wishes to the blue bird and his sister reawakens. The two find an ocean at the end of the world and begin rebuilding the world.
  • The Lunacy of Duke Venomania: Duke Venomania obtains a power to charm women by contracting with a demon and builds up a harem. Wanting to forget the face everyone bullied, he burns his old portraits and embraces his former childhood friend who once mocked him. Venomania welcomes another woman into his harem only to be stabbed. The woman reveals herself to be a man in disguise who came to save his love. Venomania collapses and the women flee the mansion. The last one to leave is his childhood friend, who he fails to confess to.
  • Twiright Prank: A brother and sister encounter a hungry demon on the beach after hiding a small box in the sand. The boy offers his snack to the demon after the girl refuses to, and the demon tells him a secret of the sea they're at, which he promises to tell his sister one day.
  • The Daughter of Evil: Clôture of Yellow:
  • The Flames of Yellow Phosphorus: A girl sells matches in order to get a loaf of bread, but nobody looks at her. She lights a match to keep herself warm but is soon returned back to reality. Her father won't feed her until she sells all the matches and in her hunger she lights her house on fire. She takes coins from the house and buys a loaf of bread. She is then burned at the stake for her crimes.
  • Madam Merry-Go-Round: madam merry-go-round welcomes visitors to her utopia and escorts them around the amusement park. She says she'll be here even if the world is destroyed and asks not to be blamed if you can't return home.
  • Evil Food Eater Conchita (revised) PV: Banica is born in 296 to Muzuri Conchita. At age 15 she is engaged to a Marlon prince, only for the engagement to be broken off the next year. Banica becomes known as a great gourmand by age 19, and starts going on a trips to foreign countries to sample more dishes. She succeeds her father in 321 after he dies and becomes the next duke. Two years later she falls on the brink of death only to miraculously recover after three days. In 325 her chef goes missing and rumors spread that she's become a cannibal. Elluka Clockworker is tasked with investigating, and finds the mansion was completely empty inside. The empire concludes that she escaped to a foreign country and the investigation is suspended.
  • Evils Kingdom: "Ma" welcomes a visitor to the theater and invites them to watch fifteen films about the history of the Evillious region. She describes the "original sin", the outbreak of the seven deadly sins, the quest to collect them, the Venomania incident, the disappearance of Banica Conchita, the Green Hunting and the Lucifenian revolution, the Enbizaka murders, the Levianta civil war, and the theater itself. The booklet concludes with a timeline of events from EC 001 to EC 999.
  • The Song that Resounds with Lulila, Lulila: The narrators cryptically describe the deadly sin's forms and vessels.
  • Red Shoe Parade: A parade of people wearing red shoes steal from thieves before they can steal from them. They are captured and released while wearing red-hot shoes, walking until they burn out.
