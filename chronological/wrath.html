This timeline is a work in progress and may be missing information from certain sources.

EC 983

  • Ma inherits Gallerian's fortune after his death, including the vessels of deadly sin he's collected.{% cite evils-kingdom %}{% cite entracte-of-evil %}{% cite dsoe-evil-food-eater-conchita %}{% cite waltz-of-evil %}

EC 984

  • Jorm Zusco dies during the Leviantan revolt.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 985

  • Nemesis Sudou is released from prison.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}
  • Muspell Freezis dies.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

Ec 986

  • Gammon Octo promotes Nemesis Sudou to the position of vice-leader of the Tasan party.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 989

  • Nemesis Sudou becomes the head of the Tasan Party after Gammon Octo goes missing.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}{% cite muzzle-of-nemesis-album %}
  • The Tasan party achieves majority control of Elphegort.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}
  • Hanma Baldured dies in Yatski.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 990

  • The rumor that Gallerian's inheritance is hidden in the Millennium Tree Forest begins to spread, and treasure hunters begin to go missing.{% cite evils-kingdom %}{% cite entracte-of-evil %}{% cite dsoe-evil-food-eater-conchita %}{% cite waltz-of-evil %}{% cite schedule-book-2013 %}

EC 991

  • Elphegort proposes annexing Asmodean and the government puts the matter to a public vote, with annexation barely winning.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 992

  • Asmodean merges with Elphegort, and the new country is named Tasan Elphegort.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 993

  • Tasan Elphegort declares war upon and invades the Republic of Lucifenia, resulting in Tasan Elphegort being expelled from the USE.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}{% cite muzzle-of-nemesis-album %} Beelzenia subsequently also declares war on Lucifenia.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}
  • The Great War begins across Evillious.{% cite muzzle-of-nemesis-album %}

EC 995

  • The Republic of Lucifenia is defeated by Tasan Elphegort.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 998

  • A "trial" is held in Evils Court regarding the locations of the final vessel of sin.{% cite waltz-of-evil %}
  • The Tasan party begins research on the new weapon "Punishment" to turn the tide of the war.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}
  • The prototype of "Punishment" is tested on the Millennium Tree Forest, resulting in the forest completely being burned down.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}{% cite muzzle-of-nemesis-album %}
  • The second prototype of Punishment was aimed towards the United States of Maistia, but misses and hits Jakoku's southern island of Onigashima instead.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}

EC 999

  • The USE army surrounds Tasan Elphegort and calls for a complete surrender. The next day, Nemesis Sudou fires 108 Punishments across the entire world from Tasan Elphegort, resulting in its destruction.{% cite dsoe-muzzle-of-nemesis %}
