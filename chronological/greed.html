Greed Arc Timeline

Events occuring during the Greed Arc.

This timeline is a work in progress and may be missing information from certain sources.

EC 944

  • Gallerian Marlon is born.[1]

EC 964

  • Nemesis Sudou is born.[1]

EC 978

  • Mira and Michelle Marlon die during the sinking of the Titanis.[1][2]

EC 980

  • Ma meets with Gallerian about collecting the Deadly Sin vessels.[2]

EC 982

  • Gallerian constructs Evils Theater in the Millennium Tree Forest to store the vessels he's collected.[2][3][4][5]

EC 983

  • The Leviantan civil war breaks out, resulting in the murder of Gallerian Marlon.[1][2][3][4][5][6]
