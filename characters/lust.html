Characters associated with Sateriasis Venomania and the Venomania Event.

Sateriasis Venomania

サテリアジス＝ヴェノマニアSATERIAJISU=VENOMANIA

One of Beelzenia's five dukes and the instigator of the Venomania Event. He is represented by Camui Gackpo.

Gumina Glassred

グミナ＝グラスレッドGUMINA=GURASUREDDO

A Beelzenian aristocrat and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by GUMI.

Mikulia Greeonio

ミクリア＝グリオニオMIKURIA=GURIONIO

An Asmodean peasant and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.

Lukana Octo

ルカーナ＝オクトRUKAANA=OKUTO

An Asmodean tailor and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Lilien Turner

リリエン＝ターナRIRIEN=TAANA

An Asmodean baker and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by Lily.

Maylis Beelzenia

メイリス＝ベルゼニアMEIRISU=BERUZENIA

A Beelzenian princess and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by MEIKO.

Yufina Marlon

ユフィーナ＝マーロンYUFIINA=MAARON

The Queen of Marlon in an affair with Karchess and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by Yufu Sekka.

Hakua Netsuma

ハクア＝ネツマHAKUA=NETSUMA

The older sister of Haru Netsuma and a member of Sateriasis's harem. She is represented by Yowane Haku.

Elluka Clockworker

エルルカ＝クロックワーカーERURUKA=KUROKKUWAAKAA

A sorceress asked by the Beelzenian Imperial Family to investigate the missing women in Asmodean. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Karchess Crim

カーチェス＝クリムKAACHESU=KURIMU

A Marlon Count in an affair with Yufina. He is represented by KAITO.

I.R.

アイアールAI AARU

A red cat possessing the body of Haru Netsuma (ハル＝ネツマHARU=NETSUMA) who gave Sateriasis the vessel of Lust.
