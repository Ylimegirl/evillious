Characters associated with Margarita Blankenheim and the organization Pere Noël.

Margarita Blankenheim

マルガリータ＝ブランケンハイムMARUGARIITA=BURANKENHAIMU

A doctor's daughter, a member of Pere Noël codenamed "Third Sleep Princess", and wife of Kaspar. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.

Kaspar Blankenheim

カスパル＝ブランケンハイムKASUPARU=BURANKENHAIMU

The marquis of Toragay, a member of Pere Noël codenamed "Second Dealer", and husband of Margarita. He is represented by KAITO.

Hanne Lorre

ハンネ＝ローレHANNE=ROORE

An investigative reporter for the Schuburg newspaper and Heidemarie's sister. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Heidemarie Lorre

ハイデマリー＝ローレHAIDEMARII=ROORE

An officer of the world police and Hanne's sister. She is represented by GUMI.

Julia Abelard

ジュリア＝アベラールJURIA=ABERAARU

The leader of Pere Noël codenamed "First Santa Claus" and adoptive mother of Lemy. She is represented by MEIKO.

Gatt Coloumb

ガット＝クーロンGATTO=KUURON

A member of Pere Noël codenamed "Sixth Venom". He is represented by Camui Gackpo.

Lemy Abelard

レミー＝アベラールREMII=ABERAARU

The adoptive son of Julia and a member of Pere Noël codenamed "Fifth Pierrot". He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Ney

ネイNEI

Lemy's other self who only he can hear. She is represented by Kagamine Rin.

Elluka Clockworker

エルルカ＝クロックワーカーERURUKA=KUROKKUWAAKAA

A member of Pere Noël codenamed "Seventh Magician". She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Gumillia

グーミリアGUUMIRIA

Elluka's apprentice and member of Pere Noël codenamed "Eighth Sniper". She is represented by GUMI.

Rin Chan

リン＝チャンRIN=CHAN

A Lucifenian diva popular for her singing and resemblance to the "Daughter of Evil". She is represented by Kagamine Rin.
