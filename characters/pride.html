The Evillious Chronicles:
Pride Arc Characters

Once upon a time, reigning at the top
Of a fiendish kingdom was a noble girl of age 14.
—The Daughter of Evil

Characters associated with Riliane Lucifen d'Autriche and the Conspiracy of Evil.

Arth Lucifen d'Autriche

アルス＝ルシフェン＝ドートゥリシュARUSU=RUSHIFEN=DOOTURISHU

The king of Lucifenia, husband of Anne, and father of Riliane and Alexiel. He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Anne Lucifen d'Autriche

アンネ＝ルシフェン＝ドートゥリシュANNE=RUSHIFEN=DOOTURISHU

The queen of Lucifenia, wife of Arth, and mother of Riliane and Alexiel. She is represented by Sweet ANN.

Prim Marlon

プリム＝マーロンPURIMU=MAARON

The queen of Marlon and mother of Kyle. She is represented by Prima.

Abyss I.R.

アビスＩ．Ｒ．ABISU I.R.

Marlon's court mage.

Leonhart Avadonia

レオンハルト＝アヴァドニアREONHARUTO=AVADONIA

The captain of the Royal Guard, one of the three heroes, and adoptive father of Germaine and Allen. He is represented by LE♂N.

Mariam Phutapié

マリアム=フタピエMARIAMU=FUTAPIE

The head maid of the Lucifenian palace, one of the three heroes, and adoptive mother of Ney. She is represented by MIRIAM.

Elluka Clockworker

エルルカ＝クロックワーカーERURUKA=KUROKKUWAAKAA

The Lucifenian court mage and one of the three heroes. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Keel Freezis

キール＝フリージスKIIRU=FURIIJISU

A Marlon merchant, husband of Mikina, and father of Yukina and two other children. He is represented by Hiyama Kiyoteru.

Mikina Freezis

ミキナ＝フリージスMIKINA=FURIIJISU

A Marlon aristocrat, wife of Keel, mother of Yukina and two other children, and boss of Clarith and Michaela. She is represented by SF-A2 codename miki.

Gast Venom

ガスト＝ヴェノムGASUTO=VENOMU

An Asmodean mercenary hired by Lucifenia during Riliane's reign. He is represented by Camui Gackpo.

Kyle Marlon

カイル＝マーロンKAIRU=MAARON

The Marlon prince and son of Prim. He is represented by KAITO.

Germaine Avadonia

ジェルメイヌ＝アヴァドニアJERUMEINU=AVADONIA

Leonhart's adoptive daughter and Allen's adoptive sister. She is represented by MEIKO.

Chartette Langley

シャルテット＝ラングレーSHARUTETTO=RANGUREE

A maid working in the Lucifenian palace under Riliane. She is represented by Kasane Teto.

Ney Phutapié

ネイ＝フタピエNEI=FUTAPIE

A maid working in the Lucifenian palace under Riliane and the adoptive daughter of Mariam. She is represented by Akita Neru.

Riliane Lucifen d'Autriche

リリアンヌ＝ルシフェン＝ドートゥリシュRIRIANNU=RUSHIFEN=DOOTURISHU

The young princess ruling Lucifenia, the daughter of Arth and Anne, and unknowingly Allen's twin. She is represented by Kagamine Rin.

Allen Avadonia

アレン＝アヴァドニアAREN=AVADONIA

Previously known as Prince Alexiel (アレクシルAREKUSHIRU), he is the son of Arth and Anne, the adoptive son of Leonhart, and Riliane's twin. He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Clarith

クラリスKURARISU

An Elphegortean peasant and employee of Mikina. She is represented by Yowane Haku.

Michaela

ミカエラMIKAERA

An Elphegortean girl and employee of Mikina. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.

Gumillia

グーミリアGUUMIRIA

The apprentice of Elluka. She is represented by GUMI.

Riliane "Lily" Mouchet

リリアンヌ（リリィ）＝ムーシェRIRIANNU (RIRYI)=MUUSHE

A general in Lucifenia's army. She is represented by Lily.

Yukina Freezis

ユキナ＝フリージスYUKINA=FURIIJISU

An aspiring author, the daughter of Keel and Yukina, and older sister to her two siblings. She is represented by Kaai Yuki.
