The Evillious Chronicles:
Original Sin Story Characters

A false prediction of the future,
…And so the world is being warped.
—Queen Of The Glass

Characters associated with Project "Ma", the start of the Evillious Calendar, and the Levianta Catastrophe.

Behemo

ビヒモBIHIMO

One of the four pillar gods and Levia's twin. He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Levia

レヴィアREVIA

One of the four pillar gods and Behemo's twin. She is represented by Kagamine Rin.

Held

エルドERUDO

One of the four pillar gods currently residing as a tree in an Elphegortean forest.

Sickle

シックルSHIKKURU

One of the four pillar gods currently living as a bat.

Maria Moonlit

マリア=ムーンリットMARIA=MUUNRITTO

The prophet queen of Magic Kingdom Levianta publically known of Alice Merry-Go-Round (アリス＝メリーゴーランドARISU=MERIIGOURANDO) and mother of Adam. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.

Adam Moonlit

アダム＝ムーンリットADAMU=MUUNRITTO

A scientist involved in Project "Ma", son of Maria, husband of Eve, and adoptive father of Hänsel and Gretel. He is represented by KAITO.

Eve Moonlit

イブ＝ムーンリットIBU=MUUNRITTO

The first candidate for Project "Ma", wife of Adam Moonlit, and adoptive mother of Hänsel and Gretel. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.

Seth Twiright

セト＝トワイライトSETO=TOWAIRAITO

A scientist involved in Project "Ma" and Pale's twin. He is represented by Hiyama Kiyoteru.

Gammon Loop Octopus

ガモン＝ループ＝オクトパスGAMON=RUUPU=OKUTOPASU

A leader in the senate's security force. He is represented by Camui Gackpo.

Pale Noël

ペイル＝ノエルPEIRU=NOERU

Leader of the criminal organization Apocalypse, Seth's twin, and the lover of both Meta Salmhofer and Milky Eights. He is represented by Hiyama Kiyoteru.

Meta Salmhofer

メータ＝ザルムホーファーMEETA=ZARUMUHOOFAA

Member of the criminal organization Apocalypse, the second candidate for Project "Ma", and mother of Hänsel and Gretel. She is represented by MEIKO.

Hänsel

ヘンゼルHENZERU

One of the "twins of god" birthed from Meta and the twin of Gretel. He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Gretel

グレーテルGUREETERU

One of the "twins of god" birthed from Meta and the twin of Hänsel. She is represented by Kagamine Rin.

Kiril Clockworker

キリル＝クロックワーカーKIRIRU=KUROKKUWAAKAA

A music box craftsman, brother of Irina, and fiancé of Elluka. He is represented by Hiyama Kiyoteru.

Elluka Chirclatia

エルルカ＝チルクラシアERURUKA=CHIRUKURASHIA

An ex-priestess, candidate for the seventh Project "Ma", and fiancée of Kiril. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

Irina Clockworker

イリーナ＝クロックワーカーIRIINA=KUROKKUWAAKAA

An apprentice craftsman, candidate for the seventh Project "Ma", and sister of Kiril. She is represented by Nekomura Iroha.

Milky Eights

ミルキー＝エイツMIRUKII=EITSU

A prostitute, candidate for the seventh Project "Ma", and lover of Pale Noël. She is represented by SF-A2 codename miki.

Ly Li

リィ＝リーRYI=RII

An aristocrat and candidate for the seventh Project "Ma". She is represented by Lily.
