The Evillious Chronicles:
Gluttony Arc Characters

Completely devour everything in this world,
There’s still room to go in my stomach!
—Evil Food Eater Conchita

Characters associated with Banica Conchita and her disappearance.

Banica Conchita

バニカ＝コンチータBANIKA=KONCHIITA

One of Beelzenia's five dukes and a grotesque gourmand. She is represented by MEIKO.

Pollo

ポロPORO

Banica's servant and Arte's twin. He is represented by Kagamine Len.

Arte

アルテARUTE

Banica's servant and Pollo's twin. She is represented by Kagamine Rin.

Josef Crim

ヨーゼフ＝クリムYOOZEFU=KURIMU

Banica's fifteenth chef. He is represented by KAITO.

Ron Grapple

ロン＝グラップルRON=GURAPPURU

Banica's grand chamberlain.

Juno Beelzenia

ジュノ＝ベルゼニアJUNO=BERUZENIA

The empress of the Beelzenian empire and an old friend of Banica's.

Elluka Clockworker

エルルカ＝クロックワーカーERURUKA=KUROKKUWAAKAA

A sorceress hired by Juno to investigate Banica's alleged apostasy and cannibalism. She is represented by Megurine Luka.

AB-CIR

AB(エイビー)-(-)CIR(シーアー)EIBII-SHIIAA

A mage working for a Lioness lord.

Platonic

プラトニックPURATONIKKU

A teenage thief frequently hired by AB-CIR. She is represented by Hatsune Miku.
