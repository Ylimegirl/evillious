Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Ichika.
  • The inside of the album features a short piece of prose about someone's intervention in the ending of the world.
  • The beginning of the booklet features prose about the events in Evillious after Nemesis' assassination of Gallerian Marlon.
  • The lyrics pages feature a negative number, indicating how many years before EC 999 the song occurs.
  • The ending of the booklet features a timeline of events from BT 022 to EC 999; EC 999 is labelled as "ta eschata", a Greek phrase meaning both "the doctrine of last things" and "death, judgment, heaven, and hell".
