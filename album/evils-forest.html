EVILS FOREST(エビルズ・フォレスト)IBIRUZU · FORESUTO

AKNP-0003

"Before they knew it, the people began to call the 'Millenium Tree Forest' 'EVILS FOREST'..."

mothy's third album featuring a collection of songs released online since EVILS THEATER and completely new songs released on May 9, 2010. The album is licensed through KARENT and is available on Apple Music, Amazon, and Spotify.

Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Suzunosuke; these illustrations were previously featured in the PV for Heartbeat Clocktower (F · V).
  • The album is narrated by "Ma", and expands on the story told in EVILS THEATER and prelude to forest.
  • The lyrics pages feature alternate English titles for each song alongside commentary from "Ma".
  • The end of the booklet features the same list of the seven deadly sins in EVILS THEATER; however this time "invidia" (envy) and "luxuria" (lust) are highlighted. Additionally each sin is paired up with an illustration depicting its "sinner"—Riliane from The Daughter of Evil for "superbia" (pride); Kayo Sudou from The Tailor of Enbizaka for "invidia"; a character represented by GUMI for "ira" (wrath); a character represented by Hatsune Miku for "acedia" (sloth); a character represented by KAITO for "avaritia" (greed); Banica Conchita from Evil Food Eater Conchita for "gula" (gluttony); and Sateriasis Venomania from The Lunacy of Duke Venomania for "luxuria" (lust).
