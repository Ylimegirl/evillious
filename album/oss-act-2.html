Original Sin Story ―Act 2―

原(げん)罪(ざい)物(もの)語(がたり)　(　)―(―)第(だい)2(に)幕(まく)―(―)genzai monogatari —dai ni-maku—

AKNP-0007

"Who will survive in the end?"

mothy's ninth album focusing on the second half of the Original Sin Story series. The album was released on August 12, 2013, and is currently unavailable.

Notes

  • The inside of the album features a list of characters and their associated VOCALOIDs.
  • Each lyrics page in the booklet is preceded by a page of backstory for each song.
  • The end of the booklet leads up to the start of Act 3 featured in Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.
