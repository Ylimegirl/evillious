"Once upon a time, the 'twins of god' released the symbols of the seven sins from that forest."

mothy's eleventh album themed around the songs in the Seven Deadly Sins series released on February 25, 2015. The album is available on Apple Music Japan, Amazon Japan, and Spotify.

Notes

  • The album was principally illustrated by Ichika, with the booklet also featuring an illustration by Yuu.
  • Initial purchases of the album came with the short story Seven Crimes and Punishments.
  • Initial purchases of the album included a bonus CD with new arrangements of the songs Regret Message and Re_birthday.
  • Seven Crimes and Punishments was not initially planned to be included in the album and was added last minute.
  • Along with the lyrics booklet, the album came with a booklet of relationship charts for each arc in the series.
  • Each of the primary songs for the arcs added a spoken line beginning with "さあsaa" ("now"), and each of the response songs added a spoken line beginning with "もしもmoshimo" ("if only").
  • Customers who preordered both this album and Deadly Sins of Evil: Fifth Pierrot recieved a download code for Ending Boy Hänsel on the novel's release date.
