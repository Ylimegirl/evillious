Notes

  • The album cover was illustrated by Ichika; the booklet features art by Suzunosuke, Ichika, and Rgveta.
  • Initial purchases of the album included The Song that Resounds with Lulila, Lulila as a sixteenth bonus track; its lyrics are featured on the back of the booklet.
  • The booklet contains no lyrics; they were instead printed on a seperate sheet.
  • The page for each song in the album listed a short phrase alongside commentary from "Ma".
  • The end of the booklet features a timeline spanning from the year 001 to 999, with the description for 999 burnt off.
  • The album came with many interactive elements that revealed various secrets—"Ma" introduces a word game on the page for Heartbeat Clocktower that when completed reveals her true identity as "Magician"; aligning punched illustrations over the CD reveals the words "crescent", "mirror", and "twilight" or "Blackbox" and "Solitude"; and aligning a punched illustration over the back of the booklet spells out "{% include jpn.html rom="taizai no utsuwa" jpn="たいざいのうつわ" %}" ("vessels of deadly sin").
