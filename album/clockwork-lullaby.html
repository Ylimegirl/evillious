Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Ichika.
  • The inside of the album features the song numbers of the Clockwork Lullaby series laid out into a formation of interlocking gears.
  • The beginning of the booklet features an email from TALOS to Kiril Clockwork and the ending features his reply back.
  • The lyrics pages for each song are accompanied by a phrase, the direction the song's gear turns, the other songs it is connected to, and commentary from Kiril.
