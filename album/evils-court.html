Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Ichika.
  • The album is narrated by Gammon Octo, the new "Gardener" replacing "Ma"; the beginning of the album features a piece of prose about his past.
  • The lyrics pages feature alternate English titles for each song alongside commentary from Gammon.
  • The ending of the booklet features a list of the four endings with illustrations of each of their masters; the Master of the Heavenly Yard is the only one facing away from the viewer.
