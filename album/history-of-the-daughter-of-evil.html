Notes

  • The booklet was illustrated by Ichika, and is based on her initial illustration for the PV of The Daughter of Evil.
  • The original plan for the album excluded Tree Maiden ~Millennium Wiegenlied~ and The Daughter of White due to their lengths; presumably he chose to release the album as a digital download rather than as a physical disc due to this constraint.
  • Prior to the album's release, mothy tweeted out previews of The Daughter of Evil, Tree Maiden ~Millennium Wiegenlied~, The Servant of Evil, Handbeat Clocktower, and A Hero's Armor is Always Crimson.
