An album of songs associated with the Daughter of Evil novel series released as a promotional campaign. Its release campaign began on March 23, 2012, and the album is currently unavailable.

Tracklist

  3. Evil Food Eater of Beelzenia
  4. Handbeat Clocktower

Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Ichika.
  • Each of the songs on the album were featured in their respective novel's trailer, although Clôture of Yellow's trailer featured versions of The Daughter of Evil and The Servant of Evil with vocals.
