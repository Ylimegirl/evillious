Lucifenia Trinity

AKNP-0010

"That king was born from mud, but there was hardly anyone who knew that."

mothy's thirteenth album featuring three songs about each of the Three Heroes from The Daughter of Evil series released on August 14, 2016. The album is currently available on Toranoana.

Playlist · Booklet

Tracklist

  1. A Hero's Armor is Always Crimson
  2. Neomaria of the Inverted Gravestone
  3. Swear an Oath on that Bridge
  4. A Hero's Armor is Always Crimson (Off Vocal Version)
  5. Neomaria of the Inverted Gravestone (Off Vocal Version)
  6. Swear an Oath on that Bridge (Off Vocal Version)

Notes

  • The album was illustrated by Ichika.
  • The inside of the album features a short piece of prose about Arth's past.
  • The lyrics pages feature an alternative English title along with commentary about each of the heroes' pasts; the first paragraph of each commentary is about Arth instead.
