The Evillious Chronicles:
Art Credits

A list of the various artists who have worked with mothy and where their work has been featured.

Ayumi Kasai (笠(かさ)井(い)あゆみ(あゆみ)kasai ayumi)

Ayumi Kasai contributed additional illustrations for Entr'acte of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Worldguide and Epic of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Fanbook. She then created the illustrations and an additional color spread for Deadly Sins of Evil: The Lunacy of Duke Venomania, and an additional illustration for Deadly Sins of Evil: Evil Food Eater Conchita.

CAFFEIN (カフェインKAFEIN)

CAFFEIN is most well-known as the creator of Yowane Haku. He illustrated "Novelette of White" and "Retrouver of Silver". He then left an artist comment in The Daughter of Evil: Praefacio of Blue and made an additional illustration for Epic of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Fanbook.

Daisuke Sakakibara (榊(さかき)原(ばら)大(だい)輔(すけ)sakakibara daisuke)

Daisuke Sakakibara edited the trailers for Deadly Sins of Evil: gift from the Princess who Brought Sleep, The Daughter of Evil: Act 2, Deadly Sins of Evil: Judgment of Corruption, and Deadly Sins of Evil: master of the heavenly yard.

Denki (電(でん)鬼(き)denki)

Denki's created the illustration for Madam Merry-Go-Round. She then contributed additional illustrations to Epic of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Fanbook and The Daughter of Evil: Praefacio of Blue, along with an artists comment in the latter. Her illustration was then used in Handbeat Clocktower.

Website · Twitter · Pixiv · piapro

DNA

DNA edited the trailers for The Daughter of Evil: Clôture of Yellow, The Daughter of Evil: Wiegenlied of Green, Entr'acte of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Worldguide, and The Daughter of Evil: Praeludium of Red. She also animated and created the PV for Handbeat Clocktower, and would later create the trailer for Quartets of Evil.

KIM DONGHOON

Maruino (マルイノMARUINO)
