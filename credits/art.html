Ayumi Kasai ({% include jpn.html jpn="笠|井|あゆみ" furi="かさ|い|あゆみ" rom="kasai ayumi" %})

Ayumi Kasai contributed additional illustrations for Entr'acte of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Worldguide and Epic of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Fanbook. She then created the illustrations and an additional color spread for Deadly Sins of Evil: The Lunacy of Duke Venomania, and an additional illustration for Deadly Sins of Evil: Evil Food Eater Conchita.

CAFFEIN ({% include jpn.html jpn="カフェイン" rom="KAFEIN" %})

CAFFEIN is most well-known as the creator of Yowane Haku. He illustrated "Novelette of White" and "Retrouver of Silver". He then left an artist comment in The Daughter of Evil: Praefacio of Blue and made an additional illustration for Epic of Evil: The Daughter of Evil Fanbook.
