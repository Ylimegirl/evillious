The Evillious Chronicles:
Content Warnings

Content warnings for the series, starting with the series as a whole and working down through specific entries.

This series is dark and features a lot of heavy content!! The majority of these are framed negatively and none of it is glorified. Due to their nature most if not all of these warnings are spoilers.

This page is currently a work in progress, if something has no warnings listed it means I haven't checked it yet. If you're worried about specific triggers or squicks, feel free to ask me on my tumblr if something appears in the series.
