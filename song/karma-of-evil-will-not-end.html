Notes

  • As the eleventh song in the Clockwork Lullaby series, it is preceeded by Banica Concerto!! and succeeded by Song of the Third Period.
  • The song's title was previously seen at the beginning of the master of the heavenly yard PV.
  • The beginning of the song has the instrumental from Project "Ma" reversed, and the end has the instrumental from Prophet Merry-Go-Round reversed.
  • The crow sound effect from moonlit bear is used again.
  • The song's gear connects to The Song I Heard Somewhere and to the "III-2" gear.
