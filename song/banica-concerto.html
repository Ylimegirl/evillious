Notes

  • As the tenth song in the Clockwork Lullaby series, it is preceeded by Swear an Oath on that Bridge and succeeded by The Karma of Evil Will Not End.
  • The song uses motifs from master of the graveyard and Evil Food Eater Conchita.
  • The lyrics reference master of the graveyard and Evil Food Eater Conchita.
  • The lyrics listed for the song on KARENT uses "Gastronomy" instead of "Gluttonomy".
  • The song's gear connects to Capriccio Farce, Seven Crimes and Punishments, and the "WITH HIM AND 'HER'" black box.
