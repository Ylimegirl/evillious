Versions

  • Gemini of Charm: The original version sung by Kagamine Rin & Len's voice provider Asami Shimoda that was featured on The Daughter of Evil ~Gemini of Charm~ Vocal and Soundtrack.
  • VOCALOID version: A version with new instrumentals sung by Kagamine Rin & Len uploaded to niconico on August 3, 2010, and later featured on EVILS FOREST.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the VOCALOID version linked from its niconico upload.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with new instrumentals and vocals featured on Evils Kingdom and HISTORY OF The Daughter of Evil.

Notes

  • The PV was illustrated by Ichika.
  • mothy wrote that he spelled the English's song title as Twiright instead of Twilight because of the duality of the characters' VOCALOIDs, Kagamine Rin and Len, as well as a pormanteau of "twist" and "right".
  • The song was originally composed for a fanmade The Daughter of Evil musical adaption.
  • While the lyrics in the album booklets use kanji, the PV's subtitles exclusively use hiragana and katakana.
  • The secret the demon tells Allen is the one used by Riliane in regret message.
  • A silhouetted figure resembling the master of the graveyard from Heartbeat Clocktower is visible in the PV.
  • The niconico upload is described as "'The Daughter of Evil' and 'the Servant of Evil'. A small memory of the so-called twins when they were young."
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 491 and describes it as "An incident during the youth of the Lucifenian princess and prince."
