moonlit bear

"First Episode"

"For on a dark night such as this, a scary bear comes out."
MK

The story of Eve Moonlit finding a pair of red fruits in the forest.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song uploaded to niconico on June 22, 2009, and to piapro on June 26, 2009. It was later featured on prelude to forest and EVILS FOREST.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original arrangement uploaded to piapro on June 26, 2009.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with revised vocals and instrumentals featured on Evils Kingdom.
  • New Version: A version with revised vocals and instrumentals featured on Original Sin Story ―Act 1―.
  • New instrumental: An instrumental arrange of the New Version featured on Original Sin Story ―Act 1―.
  • Original Sin Story version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals featured on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.

Albums

Notes

  • The PV was illustrated by Suzunosuke.
  • As the fifth song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by Escape of Salmhofer the Witch and succeeded by Barisol's Child is an Only Child.
  • The lyrics reference Tale of Abandonment on a Moonlit Night.
  • The niconico upload is described as "The tale of an encounter with a bear in a forest one day."
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 001 and describes it as "The unknown whereabouts of the 'Twins of God'."
