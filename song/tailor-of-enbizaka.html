The Tailor of Enbizaka

円(えん)尾(び)坂(ざか)の(の)仕(し)立(た)屋(てや)enbizaka no shitateya

"Leviathan Slope"

"But I have to keep at my work."
L

The story of the tailor Kayo Sudou and her unfaithful lover.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song uploaded to niconico on December 7, 2009, and to piapro on December 10, 2009. It was later featured on EVILS FOREST.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original version uploaded to piapro on December 10, 2009.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with new instrumentals and vocals featured on Evils Kingdom.
  • Seven Crimes and Punishments version: A version with new instrumentals and vocals featured on Seven Crimes and Punishments. It was later uploaded with an updated version of the original PV to YouTube on May 7, 2020.

Albums

Notes

  • The PV features illustrations by Rgveta, Jiji Fujimaru, Kurokino, Mizujun, Loft, ai, Yoiyami, Arisa, himi, Nyoho noko., Mikanfuumi, Coral, and rakug.
  • This song represents Envy in the Deadly Sins of Evil series, with The Weathered Head at Onigashima as its response song.
  • This song had its lyrics first uploaded to piapro on November 11, 2009.
  • The niconico upload is described as "The tale of a woman in love continuing with her work."
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 842 and is described as "The Enbizaka Murderer Scandal. 【Invidia】"
  • The YouTube upload is described as "Evillious year 842. A woman running a tailor shop in the eastern country of Jakoku was troubled……"
