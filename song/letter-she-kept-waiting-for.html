Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song released on Triangle –vol. 1–.
  • Reach For The Stars: A version with new vocals and instrumentals along with revised lyrics featured on EVILS EXTRA. Its PV was uploaded to niconico and YouTube on December 27, 2020.

Notes

  • The PV uses Ichika's illustrations from master of the heavenly yard, Twiright Prank, The Daughter of Evil, Seven Crimes and Punishments, That King was Born from Mud, and The Servant of Evil, along with illustrations from The Daughter of White by Satsu, Suzunosuke, and you-ring.
  • mothy stated that the song was originally a dropped plotline and was non-canon, although the Reach For The Stars version appears to have been edited to be canon.
  • The song references the lyrics to regret message.
  • mothy commented that Reach For the Stars was added to the title have it start with "Re", connecting it to regret message and Re_birthday.
  • The song was paired up with The Song that Resounds with Lulila, Lulila (Parade) in supplementary material for EVILS EXTRA.
