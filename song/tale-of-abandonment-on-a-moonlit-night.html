Tale of Abandonment on a Moonlit Night

置(お)き(き)去(ざ)り(り)月(つき)夜(よ)抄(しょう)okizari tsukiyo shou

"Insane Moonlight"

"Come, let's go meet our mother and father."
RL

The story of the twins Hänsel and Gretel being abandoned in the forest by their parents.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song uploaded to niconico on October 6, 2008, and to piapro on October 7, 2008.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original arrangement uploaded to piapro on October 7, 2008.
  • Remastered version: A remastered version of the song uploaded to piapro on January 20, 2009, and later featured on EVILS THEATER.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals featured on Evils Kingdom.
  • Original Sin Story version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals along with adjusted lyrics featured on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.

Albums

Notes

  • The PV was illustrated and created by Ichika.
  • As the eleventh song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by The Song I Heard Somewhere and succeeded by Chrono Story.
  • The song was originally going to be titled Zoo Detention (居(い)残(のこ)り(り)動(どう)物(ぶつ)園(えん)inokori doubutsuen) and had a darker tone.
  • The niconico upload is described as "Hänsel and Gretel."
  • The version featured on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition― changed the lyric "It's like Hänsel and Gretel" (まるでヘンゼルとグレーテルmaru de HENZERU to GUREETERU) to "Our names are Hänsel and Gretel" (その(その)名(な)は(は)ヘンゼル(ヘンゼル)と(と)グレーテル(グレーテル)sono na wa HENZERU to GUREETERU).
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 014 and describes it as "The discovery of Adam and Eve's corpses in their forest home."
