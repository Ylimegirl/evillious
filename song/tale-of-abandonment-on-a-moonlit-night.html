Notes

  • The PV was illustrated and created by Ichika.
  • As the eleventh song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by THe Song I Heard Somewhere and succeeded by Chrono Story.
  • The song was originally going to be titled Zoo Detention ({% include jpn.html jpn="居|残|り|動|物|園" furi="い|のこ|り|どう|ぶつ|えん" rom="inokori doubutsuen" %}) and had a darker tone.
  • The niconico upload is described as "Hänsel and Gretel."
  • The version featured on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition― changed the lyric "It's like Hänsel and Gretel" (まるでヘンゼルとグレーテル) to "Our names are Hänsel and Gretel" (その名はヘンゼルとグレーテル).
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 014 and describes it as "The discovery of Adam and Eve's corpses in their forest home."
