Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song released on Original Sin Story ―Act 1―.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original released on Original Sin Story ―Act 1―.
  • Complete Edition version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals released on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―. The first part of the song was later featured in a short PV uploaded to niconico and YouTube on December 21, 2019.

Notes

  • The short PV uses Ichika's illustrations from Original Sin Story ―Crime― and Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.
  • As the third song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by Queen Of The Glass and succeeded by Escape of Salmhofer the Witch.
  • The lyrics reference Adam Moonlit and Eve Zvezda's last names, with Adam's containing the English word for moon and Eve's being Slavic for star.
  • The lyrics listed for the song on KARENT do not match the lyrics that are sung.
  • The silhouette at the beginning of the short PV is the illustration of Sleep Princess from master of the heavenly yard.
