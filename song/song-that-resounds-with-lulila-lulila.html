The Song that Resounds with Lulila, Lulila

るりら(るりら)るりら(るりら)と(と)響(ひび)く(く)唄(うた)rurira rurira to hibiku uta

"Bonus Track"

"The moon that shines in the sky is beautiful."
RL

A cryptic song about the seven deadly sins and their assosciated items.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song released on Evils Kingdom.
  • Parade: A version with new lyrics and vocals along with additional lyrics featured on EVILS EXTRA.

Albums

Notes

  • The song references the lyrics of moonlit bear.
  • The additional lyrics in the Parade version reference the title of Miniature Garden Girl.
  • The song was paired up with Reach For The Stars ~The Letter She Kept Waiting For~ in supplementary material for EVILS EXTRA.
