Versions

  • Original: The original version released on EVILS FOREST and later uploaded to niconico on July 26, 2010.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original version linked on its niconico upload.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with new instrumentals and vocals featured on Evils Kingdom.
  • Comic version: A version with new instrumentals sent out with purchases of The Lunacy of Duke Venomania manga.
  • Seven Crimes and Punishments version: A version with new instrumentals and vocals featured on Seven Crimes and Punishments. It was later uploaded to YouTube along with an enhanced version of the original PV on February 5, 2020.
  • Symphonic: The version of the song performed by the Tokyo Philharmonic Orchestra as part of The Symphony of Evil and featured on Hatsune Miku Symphony 2023 Live at Suntory Hall.

Notes

  • The PV was illustrated by Suzunosuke.
  • This song represents Lust in the Deadly Sins of Evil series, with The Portrait Glassred Drew as its response song.
  • The women's names in the PV text are based off VOCALOIDs and some of their occupations are references to VOCALOID lore or Evillious characters with the same VOCALOID – Lukana Octo's surname references Megurine Luka's association with octopuses and her occupation is a reference to Kayo Sudou from the song The Tailor of Enbizaka; Mikulia Greeonio's surname is a reference to Hatsune Miku's assosciation with green onions and her occupation is a reference to Michaela in The Daughter of Evil series; Hakua Netsuma's surname is a reference to the glitch phrase "tsumanne" associated with Yowane Haku; Tette Cetera's name is a reference to the phrase "et cetera"; Gumina Glassred's surname is a reference to the red glasses/goggles GUMI wears; Neruneru Nerune's occupation is a reference to Ney Phutapié's role in The Daughter of Evil novels; Rindo Blum's occupation is a reference to Riliane's fate after the revolution; and Josephine Francois is a reference to Riliane's horse.
  • The YouTube upload of the PV features additional lines of text from the novel.
  • The niconico upload is described as "A tale of a man who indulged in lust."
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 136 and describes it as "The Venomania Event. 【Luxuria】"
  • The YouTube upload is described as "Evillious Year 136. In the Asmodean Region of Beelzenian Empire, there was an incident where women disappeared one by one……"
