Clockwork Lullaby

ぜんまい(ぜんまい)仕(じ)掛(か)け(け)の(の)子(こ)守(もり)唄(うた)zenmaijikake no komoriuta

"Clockwork Lullaby 1"

"This singing voice is the clockwork lullaby."
RAL

The story of a young girl singing a lullaby.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song uploaded to niconico on March 22, 2008, and to piapro on March 30, 2008.
  • Remastered version: A revised version of the song featuring new vocals and instrumentals uploaded to piapro on January 11, 2009, and later featured on EVILS THEATER.
  • Prism version: A version sung by Kagamine Rin's voice provider Asami Shimoda that was released on her album Prism.
  • Demo version: An early version of the song with different lyrics sung by Kagamine Len released on Evil Food Eater Conchita (revised).
  • Key Of Word: A revised version featuring new vocals, instrumentals, and PV along with additional lyrics uploaded to niconico and YouTube on July 27, 2018, and later featured on Clockwork Lullaby.

Albums

Notes

  • The original PV's illustration was drawn by mothy.
  • As the first song in the Clockwork Lullaby series, it is the first song to feature the complete lulila motif, and is preceded by Wordplay and succeeded by Miniature Garden Girl.
  • The first two lines of the song mirror the last two lines of Wordplay.
  • The character featured in the PV appears identical to Kagamine Rin's VOCALOID2 module.
  • mothy noted twice that the song "may or may not" be related to Wordplay.
  • The additional lyrics in Key Of Word reference the title of Miniature Garden Girl.
  • The song's gear connects to Miniature Garden Girl, Re_birthday, and Chrono Story.
