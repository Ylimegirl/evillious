Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song featured on Judgment of Corruption ~a court of greed~, EVILS COURT, and Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.
  • Clockworker: A version with altered lyrics and a new instrumental sung by Hiyama Kiyoteru that was featured on Original Sin Story ―Act 2―.
  • Clockworker Instrumental: An instrumental of the Clockworker version featured on Original Sin Story ―Act 2―.

Notes

  • As the ninth song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by Whereabouts of the Miracle and succeeded by The Song I Heard Somewhere.
  • mothy listed the song as "Clockwork Lullaby R", likely due to its use of the lulila motif.
  • The iconography on the lyrics page for the song in Judgment of Corruption ~a court of greed~ indicates that the song takes place in Levianta.
