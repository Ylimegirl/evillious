The Song I Heard Somewhere

どこか(どこか)で(で)聞(き)いた(いた)唄(うた)dokoka de kiita uta

"Clockwork Lullaby 8"

"Yes, this was formerly such a song."
I

The story of Irina Clockworker being saved by Seth Twiright after the Levianta Catastrophe.

Lyrics

Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song released on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition― and featured on Clockwork Lullaby. It was uploaded with a PV to niconico and YouTube on August 24, 2020.

Albums

Notes

  • As the eighth song in the Clockwork Lullaby series, it is preceeded by Seven Crimes and Punishments and succeeded by Swear an Oath on that Bridge.
  • As the tenth song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by Recollective Musicbox and succeeded by Tale of Abandonment on a Moonlit Night.
  • The song uses the same melody as Full Moon Laboratory.
  • The song references the lyrics of Re_birthday and Full Moon Laboratory.
  • The PV uses the same gear illustration featured in Clockwork Lullaby -Key Of Word- and in the Clockwork Lullaby album.
  • The song's gear connects to Miniature Garden Girl and The Karma of Evil Will Not End.
