Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song uploaded to piapro on April 4, 2008, and to niconico on April 6, 2008.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original arrangement uploaded to piapro on May 1, 2008.
  • act2 version: A version featuring vocals from Kagamine Rin act2 and the same PV uploaded to niconico on July 28, 2008, and to piapro on August 23, 2008.
  • Remastered version: A remastered version of the song with new vocals featured on EVILS THEATER.
  • ~velvet mix~: A version with new vocals and instrumentals uploaded to niconico on December 27, 2009, and to piapro on January 16, 2010. It was then featured on prelude to forest.
  • ~velvet mix~ instrumental: An instrumental of the ~velvet mix~ uploaded to piapro on January 16, 2010.
  • Gemini of Charm version: A version sung by Kagamine Rin's voice provider Asami Shimoda that was featured on The Daughter of Evil ~Gemini of Charm~ Vocal and Soundtrack.
  • Evils Kingdom version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals featured on Evils Kingdom.
  • The Daughter and Servant of Evil: An instrumental version mixed with The Servant of Evil featured on Four Melodies of Evil ~The Daughter of Evil Novel Music Collection~ and The Daughter of Evil: BGM Reading Collection.
  • Arrange version: An arranged instrumental by Travolta-P featured on The Daughter of Evil: BGM Reading Collection.
  • Seven Crimes and Punishments version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals featured on Seven Crimes and Punishments and later uploaded to YouTube with a remastered version of the original PV on December 4, 2019. It was then featured on HISTORY OF The Daughter of Evil and Waltz of the Deceased: Deadly Sins of Evil SEKAI EDITION+.

Notes

  • The original PV's illustration was drawn by Ichika, and the ~velvet mix~ PV featured illustrations by hal-brando, Enyukari, Nako, karenxxx, Aoi Kouta, Ryuno, mkk, ishisa, Inami, Coral, cekit, Jyu., Machico Chino, carl, Mota, Kanejiro, Halcyon, Niwa, ★Lonom☆, jonejung, Eruko ＾ω＾, Kurosaki Nekozu, Kaden, Murio, Shigeru Sawado, Mayokichi, Kuriiro, Mokomoko, Shimamura, kana_ria, Murai Aria, remaco, and Hijiki.
  • This song represents Pride in the Deadly Sins of Evil series, with The Servant of Evil as its response song.
  • mothy described the song as having a Showa-inspired melody.
  • Mothy was inspired by Satoko from Higurashi When They Cry to make Riliane's laugh.
  • The ~velvet mix~ PV for the song features various terms, with the ~velvet mix~ PV for The Servant of Evil listing quotes assosciated with the terms.
  • The piapro upload is described as "A medieval-narrative style composition with a different flavor.".
  • The original niconico upload is described as "A song depicting the story of the noble girl who became the ruler of a certain historically prosperous kingdom once upon a time."
  • mothy's official playlist lists the song as occuring in EC 500 and describes it as "The Lucifenian Revolution. 【superbia】"
  • The YouTube upload is described as "The story of the noble girl who became the ruler of the glorious kingdom of Lucifenia once upon a time."
