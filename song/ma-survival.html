Versions

  • Original: The original version of the song released on Original Sin Story ―Act 2―.
  • Instrumental: An instrumental of the original featured on Original Sin Story ―Act 2―.
  • Complete Edition version: A version with new vocals and instrumentals featured on Original Sin Story ―Complete Edition―.
  • PV version: A version with adjusted instrumentals uploaded to niconico and YouTube on May 4, 2016.

Notes

  • The PV was illustrated by Ichika.
  • As the seventh song in the Original Sin Story series, it is preceeded by Barisol's Child is an Only Child and succeeded by Whereabouts of the Miracle.
  • The original version of the song features the sound effect of a door slamming at the end.
  • The melody from Recollective Musicbox is used as a leitmotif.
  • The YouTube upload of the song's PV was age-restricted to viewers 18 and older as of March 8, 2023, due to the depictions of Milky being hanged.
